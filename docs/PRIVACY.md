# Privacy Policy & Research Intent

## Academic and Non-Commercial Intent
Sentinel-V is a specialized analytical tool developed strictly for academic research and market analysis purposes. Our primary objective is to study the structural patterns of high-performance content within the B2B sector on YouTube. 

## Data Handling
- **No Personal Data Collection**: We do not collect, store, or process any personal data of YouTube users or creators.
- **Raw Data Exclusion**: Raw database harvests are excluded from our version control and permanent storage to ensure compliance with data minimization principles.
- **Analytical Focus**: Our focus is on metadata (view counts, subscriber ratios, and title structures) to identify performance outliers.

## Compliance
We strictly adhere to the YouTube API Services Terms of Service and Developer Policies. Our application uses the API only to retrieve public metadata for the aforementioned research purposes.
